WildHacks Check-In App (v1.0)

Step 1: Click on download link and follow on-screen instructions.

Download for iOS

Download for Android

Step 2 For iOS: App will appear on home screen, if you registered your UDID with Jon.

Step 2 For Android: The APK will download. Go to your downloads screen (usually by swiping down) and tap on app-release.apk when it finishes. Your phone will give you a security warning (since it's not a Google Play app), so tap on Settings when prompted, or go to Settings > Lock screen and Security, and enable Unknown sources. Then, navigate to the downloaded .apk file and tap on it again and install. The app will install.

Finally: Launch the app named WH Check-In.
